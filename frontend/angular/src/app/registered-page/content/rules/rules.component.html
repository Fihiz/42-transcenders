Pong Rules

What is Pong

Pong is one of the first computer games that was ever created, this simple "tennis like" game features two paddles and a ball, the goal is to defeat your opponent by being the first one to gain 10 points, a player gets a point once the opponent misses a ball.

How to play

Move your paddle up and down with the arrow keys. If you activate the shield option, just press the spacebar so no ball can pass during 3 seconds. It will prevent your opponent from scoring. 3 shields are available during the all game. You can see the remaining ones at the bottom of the game.
